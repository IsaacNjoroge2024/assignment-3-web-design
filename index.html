#about

Kinetic Fitness was founded in 2017 with a simple mission: to provide personalized fitness solutions that deliver real results for Kenyans. Unlike big box gyms, we focus on quality over quantity, giving each client the attention they deserve.

Our team of certified trainers brings diverse expertise in strength training, nutrition, mobility, and rehabilitation. We believe fitness should be accessible to everyone, which is why we offer various service tiers to fit different budgets and preferences.
